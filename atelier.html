A Someday Project

It started with a pile of leather under the tree.

The tools arrived at Christmas, and my husband Chris was instantly enamoured. It was the kind of new-hobby spark where you can already picture the whole workshop.

He started dreaming about the bags he'd make someday. So I started dreaming too, and did the part I love best. I gave the someday a name, a raven and peony crest, a voice, and a whole little world to live in.

Word has it the launch was meant to be summer 2026, which gives him roughly a fortnight to master an entire craft. No pressure. Until then, the brand is built and waiting, ready for the day his real work is good enough to photograph.
