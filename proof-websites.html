Websites

Websites that give people somewhere clear to land.

A website does not need to be huge to work well. It needs to explain who you are, what you offer, and what someone should do next. These builds were designed to feel like the business behind them while making the path forward easier for the people visiting.

Fitness Studio Website

Get Cronk’d Fitness Studio

A full website refresh for a boutique fitness studio in Greenwood, Nova Scotia. The new site needed to feel welcoming, energetic, and clear, while helping visitors understand the difference between personal training, group fitness, nutrition support, and specialty programs.

Behind the decisions

The structure was built around reducing confusion. Instead of treating every offer the same, the site separates drop-in classes, programs, coaching, and nutrition so new clients can quickly find the right starting point.

Farm, Homestead & Studio Website

Raven House Co.

A warm, story-led website for a rural Nova Scotia family business with several connected pieces, including the farm, homestead life, handmade goods, and studio work. The site needed to feel personal and atmospheric without becoming unclear.

Behind the decisions

The design keeps the emotional pull of the brand front and centre while giving each part of the business a place to belong. The goal was to make the experience feel calm, rooted, and easy to explore.
