About Who we are

A two-person studio in the Annapolis Valley.

We're a two-person studio — one creative, one technical. We come at the same problems from completely different directions, which is the whole point. Between us, we cover the brand, the copy, the website, the emails, the automations, and the systems underneath all of it.

Julianna

Julianna

Founder & Creative Lead

I started this studio because I kept watching small business owners pour everything into their work and then have no idea how to talk about it. The product was great, the service was great, but the website was a mess, the messaging was all over the place, and none of it matched what was actually happening inside the business.

I was a teacher for years before any of this, which is probably why I think the way I do — I'm always trying to figure out how to take something complicated and make it make sense to the person standing in front of it. I have a master's in equity, diversity, and social justice in education, which sounds unrelated until you realise that the whole degree was about asking "who is this actually for, and is it working for them?" I ask the same question about websites now.

The thing I've done in every job I've ever had is write. Content, copy, communications, brand voice — I've been doing some version of it for a long time. That's what I do here: I write the words, build the site, and figure out how a business should sound so that when someone lands on it, they get it.

Brand strategy Website & copy Content & voice Clarity & accessibility
Chris

Chris

Technical Architect

I'm the one you probably won't talk to much, which is how I like it.

I spent fifteen-plus years building and running platforms at a national scale — big systems, serious uptime requirements, the kind of work where if something breaks at 2 a.m. it's your problem. I brought that experience here because a good website or a good brand isn't worth much if the stuff behind it doesn't actually work. So I handle the automations, the integrations, the AI tools, and the general wiring that keeps a business running smoothly without adding more things for the owner to babysit.

Julianna makes it look and sound right. I make it work.

Systems architecture Automations Integrations Practical AI
