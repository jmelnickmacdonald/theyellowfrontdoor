About The people behind the door

Every business has a window. We help yours show what's inside.

You can tell a lot about a place from the street. Whether the lights are on. Whether the sign still fits. Whether the care you'd find inside shows up before someone walks in. The Yellow Front Door is a small studio that helps small businesses get that part right — so the outside finally matches the work behind it.

Getting people to the door is only half the job.

Most marketing is built to bring people closer. Far less attention goes to what they meet once they arrive — the website, the words, the reply that comes back, the follow-up that does or doesn't land. That in-market experience is where trust is actually built, and it's the part most small businesses never have time to tend.

That's the half we work on. Not against marketing — the part of it that closes the loop.

Julianna

Julianna

Founder & Creative Lead

Julianna started The Yellow Front Door for a simple reason: she kept meeting business owners doing remarkable work that the outside of their business wasn't telling anyone about.

She spent years as an educator before making the jump, and that training never really left her. It's where she learned to take something complex and tangled and make it clear — to find the through-line in a mess of ideas and hand it back in a way that finally makes sense. Her master's work focused on equity, diversity, and social justice in education, which left her with a systems-thinker's habit of asking who a thing is really for, and whether it actually works for the people meeting it.

Alongside the teaching, the through-line in every role she's held has been the same: writing, creating content, and shaping how an organization sounds and shows up. Decades of it. Now that work is the studio's — building the website, writing words that sound like you instead of like everyone, and shaping a brand that puts your best foot forward without losing what made it yours.

The instinct underneath all of it is accessibility: making the inside of a business legible from the outside, so the right people understand it, trust it, and feel welcome walking in.

Chris

Chris

Technical Architect

A good experience has to work behind the scenes too. That's where Chris comes in.

He brings more than fifteen years of building and running national-scale platforms — the kind of systems where reliability isn't a nice-to-have and the wiring has to hold under real weight. That experience is what lets the studio promise more than a polished surface: the automations that fire on time, the tools that actually talk to each other, the practical AI and integrations that make a business easier to run instead of quietly harder.

He keeps a lighter footprint here than Julianna by design, but he's the reason the systems behind the experience are built to last.
