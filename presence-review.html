Presence Review A look at the whole picture

See your business the way your clients do.

A practical review of your website, brand, and client experience from first impression to follow-up, so you know what is working, what is getting in the way, and what to fix first.

Request a Presence Review Not sure where things stand? Start with the free Scorecard.

Most businesses have never had anyone look at the whole picture at once.

The website gets updated when something breaks. The logo was made years ago by someone who is no longer around. The confirmation email a client gets after booking still has the old phone number on it. The intake form asks for things nobody ever uses. None of these things are disasters on their own, but together they add up to an experience that feels rougher than the business actually is.

The Presence Review looks at everything a client encounters, from the moment they find you to the moment they finish working with you, and gives you a clear picture of where things are doing their job and where they are quietly getting in the way.

Who this is for

This is a good fit if…

Your business has grown or shifted, but the pieces people encounter have not kept up.
You know something feels off, but you are not sure whether it is the website, the brand, the copy, or something else.
People can find you, but the path from finding you to reaching out is not as smooth as it should be.
The experience clients have after they book or buy does not match the care you put into the work itself.
You are about to invest in something and want to know what is actually worth changing first.
You want an honest outside view before deciding what to do next.

What we look at

We go through your business presence the way a client would, from the first search result to the last email they receive, and note what we find at each step.

Finding you

First impression

What someone understands in the first few seconds of landing on your site or profile, and whether it gives them a reason to keep going.

Search and discovery

How you appear when someone finds you through Google, social media, or a referral, and whether all of those first impressions tell the same story.

Visual identity

Whether your logo, colours, fonts, and photos feel consistent and current across the places people find you.

Understanding you

Messaging and voice

Whether the words on your site, profiles, and materials sound clear, specific, and like the business, or vague and interchangeable.

Clarity of what you offer

Whether someone new could quickly understand what you do, who it is for, and what to do next.

Trust and proof

How testimonials, examples, credentials, and photos are helping someone decide to reach out, or not.

Reaching out

Calls to action

Whether the next step is obvious and easy to take on every page that needs one, and whether it asks for the right thing at the right moment.

Mobile experience

How the site looks and behaves when someone finds it on their phone, which is often where the decision happens.

Contact and intake forms

Whether the forms someone fills out to reach you are clear, appropriately brief, and ask for things you actually use.

After they commit

Booking and checkout

Whether the process of hiring you, booking a time, or buying something is as smooth and confidence-building as the rest of the experience.

Confirmation and follow-up emails

What clients receive after they take a step, whether it matches the quality of the business, and whether it sets the right expectations.

Client communications

Whether the emails, reminders, and messages clients receive feel consistent, clear, and human throughout their experience with you.

What you receive

A written review of your full presence and a prioritised action plan, in plain language, with clear notes on what is working, what is not, and where to start.

  • A review covering your website, brand, and client-facing materials
  • Notes on what is already doing its job well
  • Observations on where the experience breaks down or loses confidence
  • Messaging and content recommendations
  • A look at your post-booking experience: forms, emails, and follow-up
  • A prioritised action list, ordered by what will make the most difference
  • Suggested next steps: targeted fixes, a refresh, or a larger rebuild
  • A follow-up conversation to walk through the findings together
Quoted based on the scope of your presence and review depth. Introductory pricing available during launch.
