Systems Demo

Farm Stand Automation

A Google Sheet powers a live farm stand board on the website. Scheduled reminders prompt the owner to update, and when she does, an automated email goes out to egg share subscribers. No code needed after setup.

How it works

Four steps, no logins

The system reminds the owner to update, and handles everything after that.

Step 1

Automated reminder

Two or three times a day, a scheduled ping asks the owner if the stand needs updating. Morning, midday, and after evening chores.

→

Step 2

Update the sheet

Open the sheet on your phone, change a quantity, mark something sold out. Takes 30 seconds.

→

Step 3

Website updates live

The farm stand board on the site pulls from the sheet automatically. No publishing step.

→

Step 4

Email goes out

Egg share subscribers get a formatted update with what's available and what's coming.

What this cost to build

One Google Sheet (free), one Formspree form for signups (free tier), one MailerLite automation for the email (free tier), a scheduled reminder via Zapier or Google Apps Script, and a few lines of JavaScript on the website to read the sheet. No monthly software fees. No CMS. No app. The system pings the owner two or three times a day to check if the stand needs updating. She opens the sheet on her phone, changes a number, and her customers get a clean email and a live website board.
