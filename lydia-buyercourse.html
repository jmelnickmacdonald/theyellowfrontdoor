A sample client experience, not a giant PDF.

This sample guide shows how a real estate agent can make the early buying process feel clearer, calmer, and more organized. Instead of sending scattered links, long emails, or a static PDF, the buyer gets a guided home base with simple sections, saved notes, and practical next steps.

For this demo, two sections are fully built: one onboarding page and one buyer intake form. The remaining sections are shown as planned pieces of the full guide experience.

How to explore this sample

  • Start with the guide home to see the full structure.
  • Open Working Together to see the onboarding experience.
  • Open Pin Down What Matters to see the buyer intake form.
  • The remaining sections show how the full guide could be expanded for a real client build.

Built to show the strategy, not give away the whole course.

The purpose of this sample is to show the structure, tone, design system, and client workflow. In a live build, each section could be customized to the agent’s market, process, service boundaries, and preferred client communication style.

The result is part education, part intake, and part client care system.

Your notes help make the experience more personal.

In the built sample pages, buyers can save notes on their device as they move through the guide. They can also choose to share a specific question or comment with the agent before a conversation.

That keeps the experience useful without making it feel like a heavy client portal or a full login system.

Example note: “I’m not sure how firm I am on location yet. I like the idea of being closer to town, but I also want more privacy. Can we talk through that before we narrow the search too much?”

A better way to prepare clients before the first big decision.

This kind of guided experience can be adapted for buyer onboarding, seller preparation, client intake, post-consultation follow-up, or any service where people need clarity before they can make confident decisions.
