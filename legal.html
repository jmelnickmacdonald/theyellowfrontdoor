Who we are

The Yellow Front Door is a marketing studio for small businesses, based in the Annapolis Valley, Nova Scotia, Canada. We operate at theyellowfrontdoor.ca. You can reach us at hello@theyellowfrontdoor.ca.

What information we collect

We collect personal information only when you provide it to us directly. This happens when you fill out a contact form, request a guide or resource, or complete The Scorecard. In those cases, we collect your name and email address, and any details you include in your message.

We do not collect payment information through this website. Any payments are handled through separate invoicing and payment systems.

How we use your information

We use the information you provide to respond to your inquiry, send you the resource you requested, or follow up about working together. If you opt in to our email list, we may send you occasional updates, resources, or studio news through MailerLite. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email we send.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

Third-party services

This website uses a small number of third-party tools to operate. Each has its own privacy policy.

Formspree processes the submissions from our contact forms, Scorecard, and guide requests. Your name and email pass through their servers. See Formspree's Privacy Policy.

MailerLite manages our email list and sends any emails we send to subscribers. If you opt in to our list, your name and email are stored in MailerLite. See MailerLite's Privacy Policy.

Google Analytics collects anonymous data about how visitors use this site, including which pages are viewed and how visitors arrived. This data does not identify you personally. You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on. See Google's Privacy Policy.

Vercel hosts this website and processes standard server logs, including IP addresses and browser information, as part of normal hosting operations. See Vercel's Privacy Policy.

Cookies

This site uses cookies placed by Google Analytics to track anonymous usage data. We do not use any other tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

Your rights under PIPEDA

As a Canadian business, we operate under the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA). You have the right to request access to any personal information we hold about you, ask us to correct inaccurate information, or request that we delete your information. To make any of these requests, contact us at hello@theyellowfrontdoor.ca.

Data retention

We retain your information for as long as it's needed to provide our services or maintain our business records. If you'd like your information removed from our systems, contact us and we'll take care of it.

If we make meaningful changes to this policy, we'll update the date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review it periodically.
