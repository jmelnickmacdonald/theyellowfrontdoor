Why here

This isn't a gym. It’s the place you stop starting over.

There are plenty of places to work out. Here's why people drive past all of them to come here.

It's private.

2,000 square feet, no walk-ins, no strangers on the treadmill behind you. You'll know every face in the room within a week.

It's coached, not supervised.

Every session — personal or group — has a certified trainer watching your form, adjusting your program, and pushing you the right amount. You're not left alone with a clipboard.

It's for everyone. Actually.

Ages 7 to 70+. New moms with babies strapped to their chest. Competitive athletes. People who haven't worked out in a decade. All in the same space, all respected equally.

Nine years and counting.

This isn't a fly by night, pop-up sort of thing. Rebecca built this studio in 2017 and it's still here because people keep coming back. That's the only testimonial that matters.

The owner is a world champion.

Rebecca Cronk holds the UFE Pro Wellness World Champion title. She's competed in 15+ shows across 6 federations. And she'll still get on the floor and coach your first squat.

You'll actually want to come back.

The music is good, the people are kind, nobody's judging your weights, and you'll leave every session feeling like you did something real. That's not marketing — it's what keeps the 6am crew showing up.

What We Offer

Find your thing.

Personal training, group classes, specialty programs for all ages. Your first group class is always free.

Personal Training

45-minute sessions, one-on-one or semi-private (2, 3, or 4-on-1). In studio or online via Zoom. All programs are customized based on your goals and abilities. We recommend starting with a no-charge consultation.

Free consultation

Group Fitness

One-hour classes including strength & cardio circuits, S3 Hybrid (half spin, half strength), SPYNergy spin classes, SMART recovery, and AMP'd Up high-intensity. All levels welcome — our instructors scale the intensity to you.

First class free

Nutrition Coaching

A 12-week program covering sustainable, whole-food nutrition. We'll build your strategy, review macros, create grocery lists, share meal ideas, and navigate eating out. You can't out-run a bad diet — let us help you fuel right.

12 weeks

Squats N' Tots

Get out, have fun with other new moms or dads, and work out with the cutest training partners on the planet. Sessions focus on building overall body strength — use your own bodyweight, free weights, or your baby as the weight.

Fit4Life · Active Aging 50+

Low-impact sessions focused on building strength in movements done during daily living. Circuit training, interval training, and stationary workouts using a variety of equipment. Age is truly just a number.

Mon–Sat · 11am

Adult Power Hour

Individualized programming carried out in a group setting with ongoing coaching. Your program is designed for your needs, abilities, and goals with progressive overload built in. A certified trainer is always there.

Wed & Fri · 7pm

Youth Fitness · Ages 7–12

Introducing youth to the gym in a safe, coach-led environment. Circuit training, interval training, and fitness games using bodyweight and small equipment. Guaranteed to get kids moving and having fun.

Tue & Thu · 5pm

Youth Power Hour · Ages 11+

Developing confidence and self-efficacy in the gym while still leaving space for fun. Workouts are designed to challenge participants interested in progressing while still encouraging basic movement for those less motivated.

Pilates · With Cynthia

Mind-body exercises with emphasis on breath and alignment. Strengthen and stretch every part of the body, with options for various experience and fitness levels. Every body can benefit from mindful movement.
