Three ways to work on a website.

Not every business needs a new site. Some need theirs fixed. Some want it handled entirely. Here is how we approach each one.

Already have a site?

If you're on Squarespace, WordPress, Shopify, or another platform and the whole thing feels like it's working against you, we can help. We'll clean up the layout, sharpen the copy, and make what you already have feel like it actually fits your business. You don't need to start over just because the template isn't doing you any favours.

Want something built for you?

We design and build custom sites that look like your business, not like a theme. You get a simple editing panel where you can swap photos, update text, change a price, or add something new. No code, no calling us, no risk of breaking anything. The design stays locked. The content stays yours.

Rather not think about it at all?

Some people want the keys. Some people want a driver. We can handle the build and then stick around for monthly maintenance. Content updates, seasonal swaps, new pages when you need them. You run your business. We keep the site current.

Every site we build or fix is structured so that search engines and AI tools can understand what your business does and recommend you to the right people. That is not an add-on. It is how we work.
