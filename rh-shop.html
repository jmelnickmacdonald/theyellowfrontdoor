Shop & Studio

Made here.
From what the seasons give us.

Everything we sell comes from this place — the land, the animals, or the studio at the edge of the trees.

Fresh eggs from the flock. Candles made in small batches. Seasonal goods when the garden allows. Nothing is mass-produced and nothing is shipped in. If we're selling it, it came from here.

Available now

Fresh eggs — pasture-raised, mixed flock, Greenwood area pickup.

Our chickens and ducks produce more eggs than we can use, most of the year. We sell locally to people who want to know where their food comes from. The shells come in a range of colours — cream, brown, blue-green from the Ameraucanas — washed and refrigerated.

We sell by the dozen. If you're in the Greenwood area and want a regular supply, get in touch and we'll add you to the list. Availability is seasonal — production slows in winter and picks back up in spring.

The studio

A shed at the edge of the trees. Built from salvage. Where things get made.

The studio started as a project — something to build, something to use. It's made mostly from salvaged materials: old boards, a found door, pieces from other things.

It's where candles get poured, crafts get made, and the occasional learning circle happens when the weather is right. Small, warm, and ours.

Not everything made here ends up for sale. Some of it is just the work of having hands that need to make things.

A note on availability

We sell what we have, when we have it.

This isn't a shop with consistent stock and a shipping department. Eggs are available most of the year but slow down in winter. Studio goods appear when time allows. Garden produce depends on the season.

The best way to find out what's available is to follow us on social or drop us a message. We'll always be straight with you about what we have and when it's coming.
