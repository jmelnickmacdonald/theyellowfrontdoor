Our story

We didn't plan on a farm.
The ravens had
other ideas.

In 2023 we moved to the Annapolis Valley and found ourselves owners of a nearly 200-year-old home. Six of us. No farm experience to speak of.

What started as a family finding home has become a world we love to share — fresh eggs, baby goats, handmade goods, and the everyday rhythm of a life lived close to the land.
