Who we work with

Small businesses that are good at what they do and ready to look like it.

We work with owners who are done making do — with a website that doesn't quite say it right, a brand that was slapped together fast, or copy that sounds like everyone else in the industry. People who know the outside doesn't match the inside yet.

We build websites, write the copy, design the brand, and set up the systems behind them — all as one connected thing, because a beautiful website with the wrong words still doesn't convert, and great copy on a broken system still loses people before they reach out.

Based in Nova Scotia. Working across Canada.

Our little hobby farm in the Annapolis Valley is home base, but we work with small businesses near and far. Some are just down the road, while others are across the province or on the opposite coast. Most of our work happens remotely, though we are always happy to meet over a good cup of coffee when we can, spread everything out on the table, doodle in the margins, and hash it all out together.

What doesn't change regardless of where you are: we take the time to understand your business and your customers before we touch a single thing. The website, the copy, the brand, and the systems behind them only work when they're built around something real.

How it works

Three steps, no surprises.

01

We look.

Send us your website and a handful of touchpoints: your Google listing, social profiles, print pieces, email flows, or client materials. We look at what's working and where to start.

02

We plan.

Together, we figure out what matters most and make a plan. You know exactly what you're getting, what it costs, and what happens next before we touch anything.

03

We build.

We do the work: brand, copy, website, client materials, and systems. If you want us to stay on for ongoing updates, integrations, or automations, we do that too.
