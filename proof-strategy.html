Concept · Content Strategy

Strong Ground

30-Day Social Strategy · Voice Guide · Content Calendar

Strong Ground is a fictional wellness coaching business built to demonstrate what a content strategy actually looks like in practice. The brief: a solo practitioner with a strong in-person reputation, no real social presence, and no idea where to start. We built the 30-day plan that would get her there — voice guide first, then a content calendar with post types, topics, captions, and cadence mapped across the full month.

The goal was a plan she could hand to anyone and have it sound like her, not like a template.

Concept · Email System

Lydia Nichols

Client Welcome Sequence · Referral Email · Nurture System

Most real estate agents go quiet the moment a listing is signed. We built an email system for Lydia Nichols — a fictional Annapolis Valley agent — that keeps clients informed, warm, and confident through the whole process. A five-email seller onboarding sequence plus a referral email designed to generate warm introductions without feeling like an ask.

Every email is written to feel like it came from Lydia specifically — her voice, her market, her values. The referral email is live as a standalone page.
