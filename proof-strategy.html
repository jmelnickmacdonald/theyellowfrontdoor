Raven House Co.

One spreadsheet runs the whole stand

A working homestead selling eggs, soap, candles, and garden goods. The owner needed to update what was available without logging into anything, and a way to let egg share subscribers know when the stand was restocked. We built it on a Google Sheet. She updates one spreadsheet on her phone between morning chores, and two things happen automatically: the farm stand board on the website refreshes with current inventory, and subscribers get a formatted email with what's available. No CMS, no app, no monthly fees.
