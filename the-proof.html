Fin & Oak Bark Bakery

Brand identity · Website · Voice · In-market system

A mom-and-daughter pet treat bakery launching in the Annapolis Valley, built from the ground up with the whole in-market experience in mind. The brief was clear: warm, honest, a little chaotic in the best possible way, and nothing that looks like it came from a template.

The work covered the brand identity, the brand book, the full website, the voice and tone, and the customer-facing communications. The result sounds and feels exactly like the business behind it — because that's the only measure that matters.

Raven House Atelier

Brand world · Art direction · Voice · Lookbook page

A speculative brand built for a craft that doesn't exist yet. When a new leatherworking hobby arrived as a pile of tools, the response was to give the "someday" a name, a raven-and-peony crest, a voice, and a whole little world to live in — long before the first bag was ever made.

The work covered the brand concept, the art direction, the named collection and its copy, and a full lookbook page. Every product image was AI-generated as a stand-in until the real leather is ready to photograph — a deliberate, openly disclosed choice that lets the brand exist while the maker catches up to it.
