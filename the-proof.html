Featured project

Fin & Oak Bark Bakery

Brand identity · Website · Voice · In-market system

A mom-and-daughter pet treat bakery launching in the Annapolis Valley — built from the ground up with the full in-market experience in mind. The brief was clear: warm, honest, a little chaotic in the best possible way, and nothing that looks like it came from a template.

The work covered the brand identity, the brand book, the full website, the voice and tone, and the customer-facing communications. The result is an in-market experience that sounds and feels exactly like the business behind it — because that's the only measure that matters.

Raven House

Landing page · Brand voice · Launch communications

Raven House is a family venture launching summer 2026 in Meadowvale, Nova Scotia. The landing page was built to establish presence before the doors opened — clear, warm, and specific enough to feel like a real place rather than a coming-soon placeholder.

The work included the landing page build, the voice direction, and the early email capture experience. The goal was to make the first impression feel as considered as the business itself.
