Fin & Oak Bark Bakery

Client

Brand Identity · Website · Voice · In-Market System

A mom-and-daughter pet treat bakery launching in the Annapolis Valley, built from the ground up with the whole in-market experience in mind. The brief was clear: warm, honest, a little chaotic in the best possible way, and nothing that looks like it came from a template.

They had a name and a kitchen. They needed everything else — the brand, the site, the voice, and a system that worked once people actually found them.

The work covered the brand identity, a full brand book, the website, the voice and tone guidelines, and the customer-facing communications — from welcome emails to order confirmations. Everything was designed to sound and feel exactly like the business behind it, because that's the only measure that matters.

Brand Book Website Voice + Tone Customer Communications Visual Identity

The result sounds and feels exactly like the business behind it — warm, personal, a little messy in the best way. The brand book captures the full system; the site is live and taking orders.

Copper + Bean

Concept Piece

Voice & Messaging · Brand Identity · Landing Page

A sharp, modern coffee bar and light-lunch spot in Halifax's north end — run by a blended family couple who left corporate after the kids flew the coop. The brief we wrote for ourselves: build the full brand pipeline from voice to screen for a business that's confident about what it does, not pretentious about how it says it.

Copper + Bean is a concept piece, designed to show how the thinking connects to the output when you work through voice, identity, and execution as one continuous thread.

We started with the voice — who these people are, how they'd talk, what they'd never say. Then built the visual identity around it: logo explorations, font pairings, colour palettes, and a recommended direction. Then put it all into action with a full one-page landing site that reads exactly the way the voice guide says it should.

Voice & Messaging Guide Logo Suite (3 Marks) Font Pairings Colour Palettes Landing Page

Three deliverables, one connected thread. The voice guide defines how the brand sounds; the identity exploration shows how it looks; the landing page puts both into action with real copy, real layout, and real photography.
