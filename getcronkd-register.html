Fit4Life · Active Aging 50+

Mon – Sat · 11:00 AM · 45 min · with Becky

Sessions are low impact with a focus on building strength in movements done during activities of daily living. All exercises can be modified based on your fitness experience, mobility, and strength. You'll work through circuit training, interval training, and stationary workouts using dumbbells, resistance bands, weight machines, kettlebells, Lebert bars, TRX, cardio steps, cable machines, and bodyweight. These classes are a great reminder that age is truly just a number.

FormatCircuit, interval & stationary — modified to your level
EquipmentDumbbells, bands, kettlebells, TRX, cable machines, bodyweight
Ideal forAnyone 50+ looking to build functional strength, improve mobility, or get back into a routine

Squats N' Tots

Tue & Thu · 10:00 AM · 45 min · with Rebecca

A wonderful opportunity to get out and have some fun with other new moms or dads in a safe and welcoming space. As an added bonus, you get to workout with the cutest training partners on the planet. Sessions focus on building overall body strength using various training techniques and tools. You'll have the option of using your own bodyweight, free weights, or your baby as the weight.

FormatFull-body strength training — bring your little one along
EquipmentBodyweight, free weights, and occasionally your baby
Ideal forNew moms and dads who want to train without needing childcare

Adult Power Hour

Wed & Fri · 7:00 – 8:30 PM · with Rebecca

A strength-oriented group program where you get individualized programming carried out in a group setting with ongoing coaching. Your program is designed specifically for you based on your needs, abilities, experience, and goals, and it gets adjusted on an ongoing basis with progressive overload built in. This isn't a one-size-fits-all workout — a certified personal trainer is always available to help you learn proper lifting technique, prevent injury, and get the most out of every session.

FormatIndividualized strength programming in a coached group setting
Duration90 minutes — longer sessions for serious strength work
Ideal forAnyone who wants a personal program without the personal training price tag

Youth Fitness · Ages 7–12

Tue & Thu · 5:00 PM · 45 min · with Jacob

Kemptville's longest-running youth fitness program. This program focuses on introducing youth to a gym setting in a safe, controlled, coach-led environment. Workouts are delivered through circuit training, interval training, and fitness games using bodyweight and small equipment. Sessions are guaranteed to get kids moving and having fun — and building habits that stick.

FormatCircuits, intervals, and fitness games — coach-led, always supervised
EquipmentBodyweight and small equipment — age-appropriate, nothing intimidating
Ideal forKids aged 7–12 who need to move, build confidence, and have fun doing it

Youth Power Hour · Ages 11+

Tue & Thu · 4:00 PM · 45 min · with Jacob

Focused on developing confidence and self-efficacy in a gym setting while still leaving space for fun. Workouts are designed to challenge participants who are interested in progressing while still encouraging basic movement for those who are less motivated. You'll work with dumbbells, resistance bands, barbells, weight machines, kettlebells, Lebert bars, TRX, cardio steps, cable machines, and bodyweight in a safe, coach-led environment.

FormatStrength and conditioning — scaled for teens, coached throughout
EquipmentFull gym equipment — barbells, machines, dumbbells, bands, TRX
Ideal forTeens 11+ who want to build real gym confidence and learn to lift properly

Pilates · with Cynthia

Wed · 5:15 PM · 60 min

Pilates is a mind-body form of exercise with emphasis on breath and alignment. In each class you'll strengthen and stretch every part of the body, with options provided for various experience and fitness levels. Props may be incorporated to add variety. Every body can benefit from this form of mindful movement — it's restorative, challenging, and leaves you feeling longer and looser than when you walked in.

FormatMind-body work — breath, alignment, controlled movement
EquipmentMat-based with optional props
Ideal forAnyone looking for flexibility, core strength, and active recovery
